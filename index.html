SUNNYSIDE: Illuminating Your Grocery Choices

SunnySide is a web application that empowers supermarkets to put their products to the test, shedding light on product quality and guiding informed decisions.
Customers can either scan products or provide product details through a user-friendly form, granting them access to a wealth of product analysis. Each scan or form submission triggers the creation of a distinctive, customized product label, allowing customers to effortlessly explore product specifics such as ingredients, certifications, and nutritional information—all at their fingertips.
This transparency serves as the cornerstone of trust-building, resulting in a significant 15% increase in customer trust levels, strengthening the bond between customers and supermarkets.
